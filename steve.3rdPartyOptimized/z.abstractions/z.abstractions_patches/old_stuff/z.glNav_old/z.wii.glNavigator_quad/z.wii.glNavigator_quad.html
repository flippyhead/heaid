This version of the patch (z.wii.glNavigator_quad) is a surround video flight simulator controlled via the Wii remote. 

Viewports into the 3D space are positioned as follows from left to right: front view, right view, back view, left view



MOVEMENT CONTROLS:

 

Tilt the Wii left/right/up/down to look around and steer your movement

Use the Wii's 'd-pad' to move forward/backward/left/right (combine the arrows for diagonal movement)


Change the camera speed, rotation speed, easing, regular and zoom lens_angle, and camera coordinates  via the 1st inlet.

PLEASE SEE THE EXAMPLE PATCH FOR MORE INSTRUCTIONS ON MESSAGE FORMAT AND OTHER CRUCIAL DETAILS.

 


OTHER CONTROLS:

 

'HOME' button toggles navigation mode on/off (off by default)

-movement and zooming can only be changed while navigation mode is on

'B' button zooms the camera view in
